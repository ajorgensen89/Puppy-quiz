Read the Rules!

Follow the rules to play the game...

  1. Enter a username
  2. Read the question and answer by clicking the box with the answer in that you think is the correct answer.
  3. The correct answer will light up in green.
  4. A wrong answer will light up red.
  5. See your score at the end.
  6. Enter any suggestions to add to the Quiz at the end of your game.
  7. Enjoy and play
